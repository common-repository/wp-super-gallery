WP Super Gallery  is a plugin that takes all your images that you upload in your posts and pages and turns them into a cool styled gallery that is easy to insert into any page with Shortcode

== Description ==

WP Super Gallery Will take your blog to another level with a very easy to use Gallery system where you can setup your images and pictures on any page or post, style it the way you want. All you got to do is upload the images into the page or post you want the gallery and use the Shortcode: [super] 

  > Full control over image sizes.
  > Ability to crop images.
  > Change wording of buttons like "Next Image" "Play slide show" etc.. 
  > Autoplay slideshow
  > And Much More..


== Installation == 

1. Upload `/wpsupergallery/` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Upload some photos to the post or page where you want the gallery
4. Place the shortcode [super] in your content
